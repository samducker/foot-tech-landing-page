We Are Not a football Team
We are a football development programme that gives your child the added practise and confidence they need to quickly improve. The sessions cater for players already in teams who want to get better, those taking their first steps into football or children who simply want to be able to join in with their friends at school.
Learn football Skills
The training is done in a group setting but focuses on the individual.
Want to get better at the guitar? Strum a guitar.
Want to get better at football? Touch the ball more.
Children at our sessions literally have 1000's of touches of the ball and they'll have chance to learn the skills and tricks of their favourite players.

Each of our groups have a set small number of players with at least 2 coaches per group. This means your child gets the attention and coaching THEY need to specifically help THEM.

Your child will work with the same coaches each week so you both have the chance to develop a positive relationship with someone who understands what your player needs from their football training.

As a parent you'll love seeing the boost in confidence, social skills and general sporting development of your child.
