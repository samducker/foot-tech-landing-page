Benefits

Physical development

The damage done by extended periods of sitting is showing no signs of slowing down and these sessions have been designed to address that. Your child will develop their fundamental movement that will aid them in sport and everyday life.

Increased wellbeing

Structured-play is at the heart of the sessions as children simply don't get as much opportunity to run, jump, throw, catch and hop around like we did when we were their age. The drills are designed to help your child get all the mental and physical benefits of playing a sport but without the competitive downsides.

What is this training?

The training combines basic athletics and gymnastic-style exercises with a mix of speed, agility and quickness (SAQ) drills which ensures a quick development in key attributes for your child.

The focus is fun and through our structured play and guided discovery you'll see such a difference in your child both physically and mentally.
